Upcoming Bluegrass Jams & Festivals (work in progress)

  • (Jam) Prospect Bar and Grill Bluegrass
    Every Sunday 16:00 – 19:00
    Location: Prospect Bar and Grill, 545 5th Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11215, USA
    Join us for a monthly Brooklyn bluegrass jam at Prospect Bar & Grill! This acoustic music circle welcomes musicians of all skill levels to play traditional and contemporary bluegrass, folk, and acoustic tunes. We focus on music as an oral tradition—no screens or sheet music—and encourage easy-to-learn songs that keep the jam flowing. All ages and backgrounds are welcome, and we ask that you respect the host, venue staff, and fellow musicians. Please order through the wait staff, tip generously, and keep personal items out of the circle and exit areas. Come enjoy live acoustic music in Brooklyn and connect with the local music community!
  • (Jam) Tones Bluegrass Jam at Sunnys Bar
    Every Saturday 20:00 – 00:00 (Except Summer Sundays)
    Location: Sunny's Bar, 253 Conover St, Brooklyn, NY 11231, USA
    Sunny’s Bluegrass Jam in Red Hook, Brooklyn is a long-standing acoustic music tradition featuring bluegrass, folk, and Americana tunes. The jam welcomes musicians of all skill levels and runs every Saturday from 8 PM to midnight, except in summer when it meets Sundays from 3–6 PM. Known for its community-driven vibe, this weekly session is perfect for learning, sharing, and enjoying live acoustic bluegrass music. Join local musicians and fans for an authentic Brooklyn bluegrass experience at Sunny’s!

  • (Festival) Old Tone Music Festival
    09/04/25-09/08/25
    Location: Cool Whisper Farm, 1011 Co Rd 21, Hillsdale, NY 12529, USA
    Experience the revival of the beloved Oldtone Music Festival, returning to Cool Whisper Farm in Hillsdale, NY, from September 4–7, 2025. This family-friendly event celebrates roots music traditions, including bluegrass, old-time, Cajun, swing, and folk. Enjoy performances from over 25 artists, including Los Texmaniacs, The Deslondes, JP Harris, and more. The festival offers a unique blend of live music, workshops, contra and square dancing, and late-night jam sessions. With its motto, “Participation is Preservation,” Oldtone emphasizes community involvement and the preservation of musical heritage. Join us for a weekend of music, culture, and camaraderie in the heart of the Hudson Valley.
  • (Jam) Monas Bluegrass
    Every Monday • 21:00 – 00:00
    Location: Mona's, 224 Avenue B Unit 14, New York, NY 10009, USA
    Mona’s Bluegrass Session in Manhattan is an advanced acoustic jam for seasoned musicians and experienced players. The evening kicks off with a house band set, setting the tone with tight, high-level bluegrass and folk tunes, before opening the floor to the jam. This session emphasizes musical skill, improvisation, and collaboration, welcoming those ready to dive into more challenging arrangements. All participants are asked to respect the host, house band, and fellow musicians. Come join Brooklyn’s advanced bluegrass community for a night of live acoustic music!
  • (Show) Billy Strings
    11/14/25-11/15/25 • 19:30 – 22:30
    Location: Prudential Center, 25 Lafayette St, Newark, NJ 07102, USA
    Don’t miss Billy Strings live at the Prudential Center. The Grammy Award-winning guitarist and bluegrass innovator is known for his high-energy concerts, virtuosic flatpicking, and genre-bending sound that blends bluegrass, rock, jam band improvisation, and Americana. Fans can expect two unforgettable nights of live music in New Jersey, featuring crowd favorites, deep jams, and songs from his latest album. Secure your tickets now for one of the most anticipated bluegrass concerts of 2025!
